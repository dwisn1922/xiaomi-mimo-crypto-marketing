# Go-To-Market Plan

## Phase 0 — Setup (Day 0-2)

- Finalisasi positioning dan disclaimer.
- Setup Telegram, Discord, X/Twitter.
- Siapkan pinned post, FAQ, rules, anti-scam warning.
- Buat 30 konten awal: meme, edukasi, explainer, announcement template.
- Buat KOL shortlist minimal 50 akun.

## Phase 1 — Community Foundation (Week 1)

Goal: komunitas terlihat hidup dan aman.

Actions:
- Daily welcome message.
- 2 edukasi thread per minggu.
- 1 community game/challenge.
- Recruit 3-5 volunteer mods.
- Mulai micro-KOL outreach.

KPI:
- 500-2,000 members awal.
- 10-15% active members.
- 50+ organic replies di X per minggu.

## Phase 2 — Narrative Push (Week 2-3)

Goal: membuat MiMo mudah dijelaskan dan mudah dishare.

Actions:
- Publish MiMo narrative thread.
- Meme contest 7 hari.
- KOL wave #1: 10-20 micro KOL.
- AMA dengan community lead.
- Launch referral/quest ringan.

KPI:
- 100K+ combined impressions.
- 1,000+ Telegram members.
- 100+ UGC/meme submissions.

## Phase 3 — KOL Expansion (Week 4-6)

Goal: scale awareness tanpa kehilangan kualitas komunitas.

Actions:
- KOL wave #2: mid-tier KOL.
- Content syndication ke TikTok/Reels/Shorts.
- Weekly recap + leaderboard.
- Partner campaign dengan komunitas crypto lain.

KPI:
- CTR campaign > 2%.
- KOL engagement rate > 3%.
- CAC/community join cost turun tiap minggu.

## Phase 4 — Retention Engine (Ongoing)

Goal: komunitas bertahan setelah hype.

Actions:
- Weekly townhall/AMA.
- Education series.
- Ambassador program.
- Transparent roadmap update.
- Monthly campaign review.

## Risk Control

- Semua konten market diberi disclaimer.
- Tidak ada janji profit.
- Jangan klaim partnership resmi Xiaomi tanpa bukti legal.
- Gunakan anti-scam bot dan pinned warning.
