# Content Pillars

## 1. Education
Tujuan: bikin beginner paham risiko dan basic Web3.

Contoh:
- Wallet safety 101.
- Apa itu liquidity?
- Cara membaca tokenomics tanpa FOMO.
- DYOR checklist.

## 2. Narrative
Tujuan: menjelaskan kenapa MiMo relevan.

Contoh:
- Mobile-first crypto culture.
- Kenapa komunitas lebih penting dari hype sesaat.
- MiMo as meme + tech identity.

## 3. Community
Tujuan: bikin member merasa ikut membangun.

Contoh:
- Member spotlight.
- Meme of the day.
- Community poll.
- Weekly leaderboard.

## 4. KOL Amplification
Tujuan: memperluas reach dengan pesan konsisten.

Contoh:
- KOL explainer thread.
- Short video reaction.
- Spaces/AMA.
- Meme repost challenge.

## 5. Trust & Safety
Tujuan: mengurangi scam dan menjaga reputasi.

Contoh:
- Jangan share seed phrase.
- Cara cek official links.
- Warning fake airdrop.
- Reminder: no guaranteed profit.

## Content Mix Mingguan

- 30% education
- 25% meme/community
- 20% narrative
- 15% KOL/partner amplification
- 10% safety/transparency
