# Brand Voice

## Personality

- Energetic
- Clear
- Meme-friendly
- Beginner-friendly
- Transparent

## Voice Rules

Use:
- Short sentences.
- Simple crypto explanations.
- Friendly community language.
- Clear disclaimers.

Avoid:
- Overpromising.
- Technical jargon without explanation.
- Aggressive shilling.
- Fake urgency.

## Example Tone

Good:
```text
MiMo fam, hari ini kita bahas wallet safety. Simpel tapi penting: seed phrase itu nyawa wallet lu. Jangan pernah share ke siapa pun.
```

Bad:
```text
Buy now before 100x guaranteed pump.
```

## Visual Direction

- Mobile-first layout.
- Bright orange/black/white optional Xiaomi-inspired palette.
- Meme templates with bold text.
- Clean CTA.

## Disclaimer Short

```text
Not financial advice. DYOR. Crypto is risky.
```

## Disclaimer ID

```text
Konten ini hanya untuk edukasi dan komunitas, bukan nasihat finansial. Lakukan riset sendiri dan pahami risiko crypto.
```
