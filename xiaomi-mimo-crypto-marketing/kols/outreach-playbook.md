# KOL Outreach System

## KOL Tiers

- Nano: 1K-10K followers, high trust, low cost.
- Micro: 10K-50K followers, best for testing.
- Mid: 50K-250K followers, campaign scale.
- Macro: 250K+, expensive, use after message-market fit.

## Scoring Formula

Score 1-5 each:

- Audience fit
- Engagement quality
- Content quality
- Crypto relevance
- Brand safety
- Cost efficiency

Priority score:

```text
(audience_fit + engagement_quality + crypto_relevance + brand_safety + cost_efficiency) / 5
```

## Outreach DM

```text
Hey [NAME], gw dari MiMo community team.

Lagi build crypto-culture campaign dengan angle mobile-first + meme + education.
Kontennya ringan, no profit promise, fokus community growth.

Mau cek rate card untuk:
- 1 X post/thread
- 1 quote RT
- optional Telegram/Discord AMA

Kalau cocok, gw kirim brief + tracking link.
```

## Campaign Brief Requirements

- Objective.
- Key message.
- Required CTA.
- Must-use disclaimer.
- Posting window.
- Tracking link.
- Asset folder.
- Approval process.

## Red Flags

Avoid KOLs who:
- Promise guaranteed pumps.
- Use fake engagement.
- Frequently promote scams/rugs.
- Refuse disclosure/disclaimer.
- Cannot provide basic analytics.
