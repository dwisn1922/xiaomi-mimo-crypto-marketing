# Compliance Checklist

## Must Have

- [ ] Disclaimer on crypto/market-related content.
- [ ] No guaranteed profit claims.
- [ ] No fake partnership/affiliation claims.
- [ ] Clear official links.
- [ ] Anti-scam warning in community.
- [ ] KOL brief includes disclosure/disclaimer.
- [ ] Paid promotions tracked and disclosed when required.

## Xiaomi / Brand Risk

Because “Xiaomi” is a real trademarked brand:

- Do not claim official Xiaomi affiliation unless legally confirmed.
- Avoid using protected logos/assets without permission.
- Use “community project inspired by mobile/tech culture” if no formal affiliation.
- Prepare takedown/rename contingency.

## Forbidden Claims

- Guaranteed ROI/profit.
- “Safe investment.”
- “Official Xiaomi token” without proof.
- “No risk.”
- “Guaranteed listing.”

## Recommended Disclaimer

```text
This is community/educational content, not financial advice. Crypto assets are risky. DYOR. No official affiliation is implied unless explicitly verified.
```
