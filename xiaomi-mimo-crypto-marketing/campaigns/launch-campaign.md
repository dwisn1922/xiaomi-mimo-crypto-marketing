# Launch Campaign: MiMo Genesis Community Sprint

## Goal

Membangun initial community traction untuk Xiaomi MiMo dalam 14 hari.

## Campaign Mechanics

Participants earn recognition by:
- Joining Telegram/Discord.
- Creating MiMo memes.
- Sharing education thread.
- Inviting quality members.
- Joining AMA/quiz.

## Timeline

### Day 1-2: Setup
- Publish intro thread.
- Pin official links and safety warning.
- Announce meme contest.

### Day 3-7: Activation
- Daily prompt.
- Micro-KOL wave.
- Community leaderboard.

### Day 8-12: Amplification
- Mid-tier KOL tests.
- Spaces/AMA.
- Partner community raid/collab.

### Day 13-14: Recap
- Winner announcement.
- Best memes showcase.
- Weekly report.
- Next campaign teaser.

## Assets Needed

- Logo/profile image.
- 5 meme templates.
- 3 X thread drafts.
- 1 KOL brief PDF/doc.
- Tracking links.

## KPI

- Telegram members.
- Discord members.
- X impressions.
- KOL engagement rate.
- UGC submissions.
- AMA attendance.

## Compliance

Every public post must avoid:
- Guaranteed profit.
- Buy/sell instruction.
- Fake official partnership claim.
- Misleading roadmap promises.
